# cats.directory — Market Analysis & Potential Report

**Prepared:** April 15, 2026  
**Status:** Pre-launch concept phase

---

## 1. WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

A cat-focused platform combining:
- **Breed Directory** — comprehensive breed profiles (personality, care, health, photos)
- **Lost & Found Cats** — community-powered lost/found cat board
- **Cat Cafés & Pet Services** — local business directory (Dreigewinnt model)
- **Breed Explorer + Community** — user galleries, discussions, breed matching

Domain: `cats.directory`  
Strength: `.directory` TLD is directly relevant, memorable, and underused.

---

## 2. MARKET OPPORTUNITY

### Cat Market Size (Germany + EU)
- Germany: ~15.2 million pet cats in ~25% of households
- EU: ~110 million cats — second most popular pet after dogs
- Germany pet industry: ~€6.5 billion/year revenue
- Cat food alone: €1.8 billion/year in Germany

### Search Demand
Cat-related keywords have enormous search volume:
- "Katzenrassen" (cat breeds) — high-volume, low-competition in German
- "Katze verloren" (lost cat) — emotionally charged, high sharing
- "Britisch Kurzhaar Pflege" — long-tail, high intent
- "Katzenzüchter [city]" — high commercial intent

German-language cat breed queries are **under-served** by quality resources. Most top results are English-language sites. A well-optimized German cat directory has strong first-mover advantage.

| Base.dk / Dog.com | Pet directory, established | Legacy traffic kings |
| iAnimal / breed-specific sites | Content + affiliate | $1K-50K/month range |

## 4. REVENUE MODEL

### Stack of 5 Revenue Streams (stacked = compound effect)

#### Stream 1: Featured Business Listings (Cat Cafés, Vets, Pet Shops)
- **Free tier:** Basic listing (name, address, photo)
- **Standard:** €9-19/month — extended listing, photos, contact form
- **Premium:** €29-49/month — homepage spotlight, category top placement, analytics
- **Expedited:** €29 one-time — skip queue, fast-track review
- *Comparable: Dreigewinnt's gewerbe model. Local pet businesses will pay for local exposure.*
- **Projected:** 20 paying businesses × €19 avg = ~€380/month by Year 2

#### Stream 2: Affiliate Marketing (Cat Products)
Commission from: cat food, litter, toys, furniture, insurance, pet travel
- Amazon Associates: 3-8% commission
- Cat-specific affiliates: Nulo, Orijen, Petplan, Embrace Pet Insurance
- Average affiliate commission: €2-15 per sale
- Projected: 500 clicks/month × 3% conversion × €8 avg = ~€120/month Year 1, grows with traffic
- *On breed pages: "Best food for British Shorthair" recommendations = high intent purchases*

#### Stream 3: Display Advertising
- Google AdSense or direct pet brand ads
- CPM rates for pet/niche content: €3-12 per 1,000 pageviews
- At 20,000 visits/month: ~€120-240/month
- At 50,000 visits/month: ~€300-600/month

#### Stream 4: Lost & Found — Lead Capture + Sponsorships
- Free to use for community (builds trust + traffic)
- Premium "urgent listing" for €5-10 (pinned, badge, social boost)
- Vet clinics / pet detectives can sponsor "found cat" alerts for their area
- *This feature alone can drive massive social sharing and brand awareness*

#### Stream 5: Premium Breed Guides (Digital Products)
- "Complete [Breed] Owner's Guide" PDF — €9-19
- Breed-specific merchandise (prints, mugs via print-on-demand)
- *Lower priority — Year 3+ territory*

## 6. COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

### First-Mover in German Cat Space
- No strong German-language cat breed directory exists
- English sites (Purina, Hills, VCA) dominate but don't serve German searchers well
- `.directory` domain is memorable and brandable

### Emotional Virality of Lost & Found
- Lost pet posts get shared 10-100x more than average content
- Each reunion story = free marketing = backlinks = social proof
- Community-driven content that grows itself

### Stacked Content Model
- Every new business listing = new indexed page
- Every new breed page = new SEO entry point
- Every lost/found post = local SEO + social sharing
- Traffic and revenue compounds across all streams simultaneously

---

## 7. KEY RISKS

1. **Time to traffic** — SEO takes 6-12 months to kick in. Need patience or ad spend.
2. **Content creation** — 100 breed profiles = significant upfront work (can use AI to draft, human to edit)
3. **Directory fatigue** — Users and businesses need a reason to come back. Need engagement features.
4. **Supabase/storage costs** — Image-heavy site, file storage costs scale with traffic
5. **Competition** — existing vet clinic websites, Petco, Chewy already own affiliate space
